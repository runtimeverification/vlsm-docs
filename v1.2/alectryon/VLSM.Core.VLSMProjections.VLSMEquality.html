VLSM Trace Equality

We can also define VLSM equality in terms of traces. When both VLSMs have the same state and label types they also share the same Trace type, and sets of traces can be compared without conversion. Then VLSM X and VLSM Y are equal if their valid_traces are exactly the same.

Definition VLSM_eq_part
  (MX MY : VLSMMachine vtype)
  (X := mk_vlsm MX) (Y := mk_vlsm MY)
  :=
  forall t : Trace,
    valid_trace_prop X t <-> valid_trace_prop Y t .
#[local] Notation VLSM_eq X Y := (VLSM_eq_part (machine X) (machine Y)).
